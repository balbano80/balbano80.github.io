About Me

I was born in Oakland, Ca. I currently reside in San Ramon, Ca. I am a HUGE Golden State Warriors fan, and while not quite as big, am also a fan of the San Jose Sharks.

I decided to take the Computer Science courses a couple years ago, because my brother had recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Math and a minor in Computer Science. He is currently working as QA for a startup Data Storage company(Cohesity) His girlfriend was working on her degree in Computer Engineering and is currently working on her Master's. At their suggestion, I decided to take the introductory course, and I enjoyed learning about, as well as using the logic to create and troubleshoot programs.

And now I am currently enrolled in the UC Berkeley Extension Coding/Web Development Bootcamp program. I took a few Computer Science courses(Intro, Object Oriented Programming, and Data Structures) at a local Community College, which the instructed language was C++. I recently did do some tinkering into JavaScript. I did have some problems grasping some of the higher order functions, that mostly dealed with array manipulation such as map, filter, and reduce. I also did a little bit of basic testing with JS Koans.
